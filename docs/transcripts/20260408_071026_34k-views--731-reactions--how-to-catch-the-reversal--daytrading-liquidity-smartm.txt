Here's how to tell if price is going to keep running or flip the other way. And under 33 seconds, start with the trend. In this case, the market is clearly bullish. We get a break of structure. With displacement, price pulls back, then another strong break higher. But notice what happens next. The pullback forms, and this time, price fails to displace higher. The candle closes with just a wick check. The higher time frame that failed, push lines up with a four-hour fair value gap. Price reacts, momentum shifts, and suddenly the bias flips bearish. That's how you know when continuation ends and reversal begins.
